Harmonia

Picking songs to sing in karaoke is stressful. Say goodbye to embarrassing voice cracks by finding your vocal range!

The computer prompts the user to sing notes on a scale. It displays the user's accuracy on a tuner and tracks the highest and lowest note sung. Keeping in mind the user's vocal range, the program will determine a suitable vocal part and provide appropriate songs.
